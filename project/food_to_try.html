Food to Try

Pho

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, usually beef or chicken. It is a popular street food in Vietnam and is often enjoyed for breakfast.

Banh Mi

Banh Mi is a Vietnamese sandwich that combines a crispy baguette with savory ingredients such as grilled pork, pate, pickled vegetables, cilantro, and chili. It reflects the fusion of French and Vietnamese culinary influences.

Banh Bao

Banh Bao is a steamed bun filled with a variety of ingredients, typically including pork, mushrooms, and quail eggs. It is a popular snack or breakfast item in Vietnam, known for its soft texture and flavorful filling.

Buc Luc Lao

Buc Luc Lao is a traditional Vietnamese dish made with stir-fried beef and vegetables, often served with rice. It is known for its savory flavors and tender meat, making it a popular choice among locals and visitors alike.

Chao Ga

Chao Ga is a Vietnamese rice porridge made with chicken, often flavored with ginger, garlic, and herbs. It is a comforting dish commonly eaten for breakfast or when feeling under the weather.

Bun Cha

Bun Cha is a Vietnamese dish consisting of grilled pork served over a bed of rice noodles, accompanied by fresh herbs and a dipping sauce. It is a popular street food in Hanoi and is known for its flavorful combination of ingredients.

Goi Cuon

Goi Cuon, also known as Vietnamese spring rolls or fresh spring rolls, are made with rice paper wrappers filled with a combination of shrimp, pork, vermicelli noodles, and fresh herbs. They are typically served with a dipping sauce and are a light and refreshing appetizer or snack.

Banh Xeo

Banh Xeo is a Vietnamese savory pancake made from rice flour, turmeric, and coconut milk, filled with ingredients such as shrimp, pork, and bean sprouts. It is typically served with fresh herbs and a dipping sauce, offering a crispy texture and flavorful taste.

Cao Lau

Cao Lau is a traditional Vietnamese noodle dish originating from Hoi An. It features thick rice noodles, slices of pork, fresh herbs, and crispy croutons, all served in a small amount of flavorful broth. The dish is known for its unique texture and rich taste.
